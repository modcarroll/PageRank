COSC 5376: Fundamentals of Data Warehouses
This course provides an overview of the fundamentals of data warehousing including
the planning, designing, building, populating, and maintaining a successful data warehouse. Specific topics covered include the logical design of a data warehouse, data warehousing architecture, extract-transform-load processing, a comparison of OLAP and OLTP and query processing utilizing multidimensional views of data. Offered in summer of even years.
Prerequisite: COSC 5360.
