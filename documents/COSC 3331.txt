COSC 3331: E-Commerce Programming
The course deals with the technical aspects of e-commerce. Students will learn to design, build, and maintain a complete e-commerce website. Topics include: e-commerce modeling, designing, and implementing a website that meets user requirements, maintaining and setting web servers, multi-tiered web architecture, database servers, accessing remote databases, shopping cart fundamentals, commerce server, advertising on the web, e-cash and electronic payments,
and Internet Security and encryption. Students will build their own projects. Offered in spring of even years.
Prerequisite: COSC 3310.
