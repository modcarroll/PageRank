COSC 4362: Computer Security
This course will give a complete coverage of cryptography, network protocols and their use in computer security. This will include an overview of symmetric and asymmetric cryptographic algorithms and their use for authentication, e-mail and e-commerce. Network security protocols covered will include Kerberos, SET and SMIME. Offered
in spring of even years.
Prerequisites: COSC 2336, and COSC 4360 or COSC 4325.
